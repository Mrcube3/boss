The Problem

Slow Execution

Manual trading requires switching between platforms, typing commands, and confirming — losing critical seconds in fast markets.

🧠

Single-Model Risk

Relying on one AI model for trade decisions creates blind spots. Different models see different signals in the same data.

📊

Context Blindness

Most trading bots ignore visual chart context. Regime classification from charts is critical for risk-aware execution.
